The problem

Low participation and limited understanding of on-chain governance in the Cardano community weaken the effectiveness of Voltaire and DRep systems. Poor participation opens doors to decisions made by a small minority and reduces decentralization in practice.

  • Only ~1.5% of staking wallets participated in the latest test vote.
  • Only ~10% of total staked ADA was used for voting.

We reference DAO failures (BadgerDAO, Solend) and community metrics to highlight the urgency for governance education.
